About Envision

We started with a simple belief: fundraising should be personal, powerful, and done right. Everything we've built since has been in service of that idea.

Our Story

Envision Fundraising was founded on a straightforward principle — that charities deserve fundraising partners who care as much about their mission as they do. What started as a small operation has grown into one of North America's most trusted face-to-face fundraising companies, operating across 20+ cities in Canada and the United States.

🎯

Mission-First

Every campaign we run starts with the charity's mission. We don't treat causes like products — we treat them like the life-changing work they are.

💪

Built Different

We invest more in training, pay more attention to compliance, and hold ourselves to a higher standard than anyone in this space. That's not marketing — it's policy.

🌍

Real Impact

Over 500,000 donors connected. Millions raised. Dozens of charities supported. The numbers are nice, but what matters is the work those dollars fund.

What We Stand For

The principles that guide every conversation, every campaign, and every hire.

Integrity

We do what we say we'll do. Our charity partners trust us with their brand, and we take that responsibility seriously — no exceptions, no shortcuts.

Excellence

Good enough isn't good enough. We obsess over quality — in our training, our donor interactions, and our reporting. Every touchpoint matters.

People First

Behind every metric is a human being — a donor, a fundraiser, a charity worker. We build relationships, not transactions.

Growth

We push ourselves and our teams to improve every single day. Complacency is the enemy, and we don't give it an inch.
