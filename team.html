Meet the Team

The people crazy enough to think fundraising should actually be fun.

John MacInnis

CEO & Founder

Imagine a Road Runner crossed with a TED Talk speaker who also happens to dress like he's perpetually about to walk into a GQ photoshoot. That's John. He runs on caffeine, charisma, and a genuine belief that every single person on his team can be better than they were yesterday. If the energy in the room is at a 6, John walks in and it's immediately an 11. Boundaries exist so he can push past them.

“Sleep is just a loading screen between wins.”

Aidan Hughes

Vice President

If a honey badger wore a suit and ran a fundraising company, it would still be less intense than Aidan. Ruthlessly strategic, unapologetically direct, and allergic to excuses — he built Envision on the radical idea that you should just be really, really good at what you do. No fluff. No shortcuts. Just results that make other companies quietly update their LinkedIn.

Dedicated, honest and always up for a challenge.
